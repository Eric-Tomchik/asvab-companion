About the AFQT Score

Your AFQT (Armed Forces Qualification Test) score determines your overall eligibility for military enlistment. It's calculated from four subtests: AR (Arithmetic Reasoning), MK (Mathematics Knowledge), WK (Word Knowledge), and PC (Paragraph Comprehension). Minimum scores vary by branch — Army/Navy: 31, Marines: 32, Air Force/Coast Guard/Space Force: 36.
