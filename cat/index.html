Computer Adaptive Test — questions get harder when you answer correctly and easier when you don't, just like the real ASVAB.

How CAT-ASVAB Works

⚡ Adaptive Difficulty Questions start at medium difficulty. Get one right → next question is harder. Get one wrong → next is easier. Your final score reflects the difficulty you reached.
🚫 No Going Back Just like the real CAT-ASVAB, once you answer a question, you cannot change it or go back.
⏱️ Timed Per Subtest Each subtest has its own time limit. If time runs out, you move to the next subtest automatically.
📊 Instant Feedback See if you got each question right immediately, with detailed explanations.
